Predators and Prey

There are many predator and prey relationships in the taiga. The wolf eats the moose and the deer and usually hunts in packs of around 3-10 wolves. This allows them to take on bigger animals than thier size. The lynx is a solitary predator. It hunts deer and smaller animals and can be very silent to sneak up on its prey. The bear will eat fish and other small mammals.
