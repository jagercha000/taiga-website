Biotic Factors

The biotic factors of the tagia are many differnent plant and animal species adapted to the cold. The ligonberry is a producer. It is eaten by deer and the moose, and thrives in the acidic soil and cold climate of the tagia. The deer is a comsumer. It consumes the ligonberries. The moose is a consumer. It eats berries, bark and twigs.The wolf and lynx are secondary consumers.
